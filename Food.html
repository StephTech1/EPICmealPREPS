Salmon with Veg and Carbs

Quick Recipe

Ingredients

    • 1 salmon fillet
    • 2 plum tomatoes
    • 1 large sweet potato
    • A selection of fresh veg
    • Salt, pepper, honey, Olive oil and garlic granules to taste

    Method

    1. Preheat oven to 180o degrees.
    2. Season Salmon with honey, salt, pepper and garlic granules.
    3. Chop baby plum tomatoes in half and place along salmon fillet.
    4. Add butter and chopped spring onions and pop in oven, uncovered for 30 mins or so.
    5. Add olive oil and himilayan pink sea salt to sweet potatoes and roast for 40 mins.
    6. Steam okra, sweetcorn and any other fresh veg to accompany the fish and potato.
